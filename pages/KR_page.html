Korean Language

Exploring the fascinating history and cultural significance of the Korean language.

History of Korean

Korean has a unique history with the creation of Hangul in the 15th century by King Sejong the Great. This scientific writing system was designed to be easy to learn and represents the sounds of the Korean language perfectly.

Cultural Significance

Korean is the language of K-pop, K-dramas, and a rich cultural heritage that spans thousands of years. The Korean Wave (Hallyu) has made Korean culture and language popular worldwide.

Learning Resources

While Korean grammar is quite different from English, Hangul is considered one of the most logical writing systems in the world. With consistent practice, learners can quickly become proficient in reading and writing.
